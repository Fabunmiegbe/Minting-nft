The Origin

Born from Obsession.

It started in a private studio in 2021. A former museum curator and a generative artist sat across a whiteboard covered in questions — not answers. What if fine art carried its own provenance, unforgeably? What if a collector in Lagos held the same verifiable claim as one at Christie's?

What followed was three years of quiet work. Eighteen months of generative research. Six months iterating the algorithm against a hand-drawn library of 4,000 source compositions. A private beta with 200 collectors who had never touched a crypto wallet before.

The collection launched silently. No marketing. No influencers. Within 72 hours, 4,200 pieces were gone — word of mouth through collector circles and one post from a hedge fund manager in Singapore who called it "the first NFT I'd hang on a real wall."

"We had 3,157 remaining when we paused for the final audit. By the time we resumed, there were already 400 people in the waitlist. We knew then — this wasn't just ours anymore."— Kairo Voss, Founder

Each of the 10,000 pieces was generated from over 200 hand-crafted base traits — no stock assets, no shortcuts. Every background, every layer, every glow effect was drawn and signed off by our art director before entering the generation pool.
